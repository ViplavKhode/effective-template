Association for Computing Machinery

The ACM (Association for Computing Machinery), founded in 1947, is the largest and oldest international scientific and industrial computer society. Through its many Special Interest Group s, the ACM fosters research and communication in a broad range of computing areas.

Why ACM and what are the benefits?

Here we need to write about benefits of joining acm.

                                                                                                          Benefits of Joining ACM
Learning

Learning Center with resources for lifelong learning, online books & videos from Skillsoft®, Safari®, Morgan Kaufmann and Syngress; videos and webinars on hot topics.

Library

Subscription to the full ACM Digital Library the Full-Text Collection of all ACM publications, including journals, conference proceedings, technical magazines, newsletters and books.

Exposure

Participation in ACM Distinguished Speakers Program (DSP) and a chance to represent your college at national level among IIT's, NIT's and some of the prestigious colleges.

Magazines

TechNews, A full-year subscription to XRDS: Crossroads, Student Magazine, A full year online subscription to Communications of the ACM.

E-mail

A free email forwarding address with high-quality spam filtering. (for example yourname@acm.org and it will always be linked to original email.)

Oppurtunities

CareerNews, ACM's twice monthly email career news digest, A full-year electronic subscription to monthly MemberNet newsletter.
